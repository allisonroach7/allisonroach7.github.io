Our Mission

The majority of our items are upcycled from non-repairable instruments and musical equipment. We believe in giving these items new life and purpose, while preserving their musical heritage.

Our creative process focuses on using as many of the original item's parts as possible, transforming them into unique functional art pieces and home decor that tell a story.

Giving Back to Music Education

A portion of all our sales are donated to the Topsail Music Program, supporting the next generation of musicians and helping keep music education alive in our community.

Have Something Special in Mind?

If you have an instrument to donate or would like something customized, please consider us for your needs. We love working on special projects and creating one-of-a-kind pieces that hold personal meaning.
